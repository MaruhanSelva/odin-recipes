Simple Creme Brulee

Description

This is a recipe for a simple creme brulee provided through allrecipes.com. It is rich and creamy and good for a small snack!

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp white sugar, divided

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Whisk 3 tbsp sugar and cream in microwave until well-combined
  3. Whisk in egg yolks and vanilla extract until smooth
  4. Pour cream into ramekins
  5. Set ramekins in pan and pour water until halfway up ramekins
  6. Bake in oven until set but slightly jiggly (50 mins)
  7. Remove ramekins from water and put in fridge for 2 hours
  8. Sprinkle sugar on dessert and lightly melt it
